About Us

Learn More About Us

Introducing Baker’s Choice Catering: A family-owned culinary venture delivering an authentic experience curated by Chef Tanesha Baker.

  • Infused with cultural influences from Jamaica, West Indies, and Cuba, Chef Tanesha brings the purest island flavors to every dish.
  • With over 15 years of experience and a culinary arts degree, Tanesha merges her formal education with a heritage of family wisdom. Drawing from lessons imparted by her mother, she creates exquisite treats that reflect a harmonious blend of tradition and innovation.
  • Beyond Jamaican classics, Baker’s Choice Catering offers a diverse range of mouthwatering delights, evoking the nostalgia of homemade favorites.

Baker’s Choice Catering, led by Chef Tanesha Baker, is your gateway to reliving cherished flavors and embracing a genuine taste of home, all while honoring her rich cultural heritage and family traditions.

Why Choose Us?

We're a heartwarming catering company driven by a dynamic mother-daughter team, sharing a passion for culinary excellence and infusing love into every dish. Our creations blend cherished family recipes with innovative culinary delights, all carefully chosen to elevate your special occasions. Whether it's an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, we bring flavors to life, crafting memorable experiences with each plate we serve. With a commitment to authenticity and a dedication to exceeding expectations, we transform events into extraordinary culinary journeys.

Family Tradition and Love

Infusing every dish with family tradition and care, our mother-daughter partnership creates heartfelt flavors and memorable moments.

Culinary Excellence
